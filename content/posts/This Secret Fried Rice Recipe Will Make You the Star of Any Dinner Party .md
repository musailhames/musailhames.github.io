## Ingredients

- 2 cups of cooked white or brown rice
- 1/2 cup of diced onions
- 1/2 cup of diced carrots
- 1/2 cup of diced celery
- 1/4 cup of frozen peas
- 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
- 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon of ginger powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Tips on how to select ingredients

When selecting ingredients for this recipe, it's important to choose fresh and high-quality ingredients. For example, select fresh vegetables and spices, and use a high-quality soy sauce.

## Steps to Make the Fried Rice

### Preparation

1. Start by heating a large skillet or wok over medium heat.
2. Add the onions, carrots, and celery to the skillet and sauté until they are tender.
3. Add the frozen peas and cooked rice to the skillet and stir to combine.

### Cooking

1. Add the soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic powder, and ginger powder to the skillet and stir to combine.
2. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the mixture is hot and the vegetables are cooked through.
3. Taste and adjust the seasonings as needed.

### Finishing touches

1. Serve the fried rice with a sprinkle of chopped green onions and a drizzle of sesame oil.
