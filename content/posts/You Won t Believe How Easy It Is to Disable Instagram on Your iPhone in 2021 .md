Here’s how to disable Instagram on your iPhone:

A. Open the Settings App

Open the Settings app on your iPhone and select “Screen Time.”

B. Select Screen Time

Once you’ve opened the Screen Time settings, select “Content & Privacy Restrictions.”

C. Select Content & Privacy Restrictions

Once you’ve selected Content & Privacy Restrictions, select “Allowed Apps.”

D. Select Allowed Apps

Once you’ve selected Allowed Apps, toggle off Instagram. This will disable the app on your iPhone. 

E. Toggle Off Instagram

Once you’ve toggled off Instagram, it will no longer appear on your Home screen or in your list of apps.
