## What Is Instagram Cache? 
Instagram cache is the temporary data that the app stores on your device to help it run more quickly and efficiently. ## Step-by-Step Guide to Clear Instagram Cache on iPhone 2022 
Now that you know the benefits of clearing your Instagram cache, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get the job done: 

1. Open Settings 
2. Select General 
3. Select iPhone Storage 
4. Select Instagram 
5. Select Delete App 
6. Reinstall Instagram App
